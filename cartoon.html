Cartoon Shows

What is a cartoon show?

A cartoon show is drawings being bought to life via animation. This can be done either digitally via computer programs, effects and the use of voice actors. Another way cartoons can be made are by physical hard copy prints of cartoons such as comic strips. Nowadays people watch cartoon shows than read books about them like Horrid Henry, Dennis the Menace and superhero stuff too. Cartoon shows as well as being a source of entertainment by giving a good laugh and time pass, it also plays an important role in how you're children interact with others around them by showing real life situations they could get into and how to resolve them.
